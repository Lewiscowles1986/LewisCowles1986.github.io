What if Diversity was not good for business?

2020-06-14

mfw i hear the "diversity is good for business" lines trotted out.

what if it *weren't* good for business? what if it cost your company money, with no ROI, to not be racist/sexist/homophobic/ageist/ableist/etc? would diversity and inclusion still be core values for your company?
