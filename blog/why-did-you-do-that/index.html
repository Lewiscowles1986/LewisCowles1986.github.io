Why did you do that?

2019-11-09

I Just posted an update on a project I've been working on today, and was asked "What was your goal?".

I wasn't clear perhaps. In my own mind things seem pretty obvious because I have instant access to my motivations and context, I can forget to share.

I updated a thing to add improvements, to ensure that it continues to work, and in-fact works better.

It's a great thing to think about why we do what we do.

Why do others do what they do is another very cool thing to think about.

How you can help others, and how what you do will help others is perhaps a wonderful north-star.
